Technology & Science Dynamics / TSD

Technology & Science Dynamics (TSD) is a research and development corporation located in Santa Barbara, CA, USA and Yerevan, Armenia with links to more than fifteen international research & development centers. TSD provides a vibrant, dynamic and multi-disciplinary research in the fields of the company specialities.We employ, co-locate and collaborate with over 55 post-doctoral research staff and PhD students currently at 27 academic, commercial, governmental organizations worldwide. TSD provides a vibrant and dynamic research and development environment and a culture that encourages inter and multi-disciplinary research. We also implement educational programs (technological training courses, internship opportunities, exchange programs, modernization of educational technologies, etc.) for young specialists and students.
